How to play hex?

Hex is a 2 player game where the objective is to build a line that connects the two sides of the board
Each player is assigned a color and will place their pieces on the board so to connect the two sides with its color
(ie. if you are the red player, your goal is to connect the two red sides of the board)
Every turn you get to place a piece until one of the players wins!
Good Luck!
